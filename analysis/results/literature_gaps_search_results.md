### Key Findings

1. **Method Sensitivity:** SSI time series and drought characteristics are **highly sensitive to method choice**
2. **Parametric Issues:** Sensitivity to both low and high ends of sample, rejection rates up to 30%
3. **Tweedie Recommendation:** 2% rejection rate, lower bound at zero — best overall parametric choice
4. **No Universal Best:** Most suitable method depends on evaluation criteria
5. **Conclusion:** "Shown approach-specific sensitivities and uncertainties should be carefully considered"

### Relevance for Paper #1

**Direct Support:** Your percentile-based MDI avoids the parametric uncertainties identified by Tijdeman et al. (2020). Your comparison against SSI (parametric, univariate) across 30 years (1991–2020) addresses the methodological uncertainty they highlighted.

**Citation Use:**

- **Section 3.1** (Percentile vs. Standardized comparison)
- **Discussion** (robustness under non-stationary conditions)
