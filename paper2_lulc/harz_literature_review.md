### 5.2 What We Don't Know (Harz Gaps)

| Gap | Why It Matters | How to Address |
|-----|----------------|----------------|
| **Beech Root Depth (Harz)** | Shallow granite soils may limit depth advantage | Soil coring, GPR (ground-penetrating radar) |
| **Interception (Harz Spruce)** | No direct measurements, extrapolated from NRW | Throughfall gauges (n=10+ sites) |
| **Drought Lags (Harz)** | Vorobevskii 2022 is Saxony (not Harz) | Cross-correlation analysis (P→SM→Q) |
| **LULC × Climate Interaction** | No Harz-specific interaction study | Counterfactual modeling (S0-S3) |
| **Multi-Year LULC (Harz)** | No CORINE-based mHM study for Harz | Busari 2021 approach (2006/2012/2018) |

**Priority Gap:** Drought lags (H4) — Vorobevskii 2022 is closest (Saxony), but not Harz-specific.
